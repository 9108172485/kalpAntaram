*Viṣṇu* (50.29, 40-41).—‘If he has killed a camel, he must give a golden
*Kṛṣṇala*. If he has killed a wild carnivorous animal, he must give a
milch cow; if a non-carnivorous wild animal, he must give a heifer.’
