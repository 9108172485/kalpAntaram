‘*Deer*’—stands for such wilder varieties as the ‘*Ruru*’ the ‘*Pṛṣata*’
and the like.

‘*Ibha*’—is *elephant*. Though the elephant also is a kind of ‘*mṛga*,’
yet it has been mentioned separately as being, among tame animals.

‘*Mīna*’—fish.

‘*Ahi*’—snake.—(68)
