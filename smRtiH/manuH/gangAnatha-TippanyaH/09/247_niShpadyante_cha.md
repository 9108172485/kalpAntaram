**(verses 9.246)  
**

This verse is quoted in *Vivādaratnākara* (p. 638);—and in
*Vyavahāra-Bālambhaṭṭī* (p. 1053).

**(verse 9.247)  
**

This verse is quoted in *Vivādaratnākara* (p. 638), which explains
‘*vikṛtam*’ as being maimed of hands, feet and so forth;—and in
*Vyavahāra-Bālambhaṭṭī* (p. 1053).
