This verse is quoted in *Vīramitrodaya* (Rājanīti, p. 202);—in
*Vīramitrodaya* (Lakṣaṇa, p. 238);—in *Nītimayūkha* (p. 65), which says

garrison, they are repulsed;—and in *Rājanītiratnākara* (p. 20a).
