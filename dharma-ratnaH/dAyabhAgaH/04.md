## 01

[[4.1.1]] atha strīdhanavibhāgārthaṃ prathamaṃ strīdhanaṃ nirūpyate |

	tatra viṣṇuḥ: "pitṛmātṛsutabhrātṛdattam adhyagnyupāgataṃ ādhivedanikaṃ bandhudattaṃ śulkānvādheyakam iti strīdhanam" [Vi 17.18] | 

[[4.1.2]] anvādheyakam āha kātyāyanaḥ |

		vivāhāt parato yat tu labdhaṃ bhartṛkulāt striyā |

		anvādheyaṃ tad uktaṃ tu labdhaṃ bandhukulāt tathā || [K 899]

		ūrdhvaṃ labdhaṃ tu yat kiṃcit saṃskārāt prītitaḥ striyā |

		bhartuḥ pitroḥ sakāśād vā anvādheyaṃ tu tad bhṛguḥ || [K 900]

[[4.1.3]] bandhupadena mātāpitor upādānam | tenāyam arthaḥ — mātāpitṛdvāreṇa saṃbandhināṃ pitroś ca sakāśād yat tu vivāhāt parato labdham | tathā bhartuḥ sakāśād bhartṛkulāc ca śvaśurādito yal labdhaṃ dhanaṃ tad anvādheyam | viṣṇuvacane ca bandhupadaṃ mātulādyabhiprāyaṃ pitrādīnāṃ svapadenaiva nirdiṣṭatvāt | pariṇayanasamayalabdhasya ca brāhmādyāsurādiviśeṣeṇa bhartuḥ pitror vādhikārāt |

[[4.1.4]] strīdhanam āhatur manu-kātyāyanau |

		adhyagnyadhyāvāhanikaṃ dattaṃ ca prītitaḥ striyai |

		bhrātṛmātṛpitṛprāptaṃ ṣaḍvidhaṃ strīdhanam smṛtam || [M 9.194 = K 894]

tathā nāradaḥ |

		adhyagnyadhyāvāhanikaṃ bhartṛdāyas tathaiva ca |

		bhrātṛdattaṃ pitṛbhyāṃ ca ṣaḍvidhaṃ strīdhanaṃ smṛtam || [N 13.8]

[[4.1.5]] etad vyākurute kātyāyanaḥ |

		vivāhakāle yat strībhyo dīyate hy agnisaṃnidhau |

		tad adhyagnikṛtaṃ sadbhiḥ strīdhanaṃ parikīrtitam || [K 895]

		yat punar labhate nārī nīyamānā hi paitṛkāt |

		adhyāvāhanikaṃ nāma tat strīdhanam udāhṛtam || [K 896]

[[4.1.6]] paitṛkād ity ekaśeṣeṇa pitṛmātṛkulād yal labhate dhanaṃ bhartṛgṛhaṃ nīyamānā tad adhyāvāhanikam |

[[4.1.7]] bhartṛdāyo bhartṛdattaṃ dhanaṃ bhartṛdāyam anabhidhāya manvādibhir bhartṛdattasyābhidhānāt | nāradenāpi bhartṛdattam anabhidhāya bhartṛdāyasyābhidhānāt | 

[[4.1.8]] tathānyatrāpi bhartṛdattae bhartṛdāyaprayogo dṛṣṭaḥ | yathā kātyāyanaḥ |

		bhartṛdāyaṃ mṛte patyau vinyaset strī yatheṣṭataḥ |

		vidyamāne tu saṃrakṣet kṣapayet tatkule 'nyathā || [K 907]

[[4.1.9]] asyārthaḥ — bhartṛdattaṃ dhanaṃ bartari mṛte yatheṣṭaṃ viniyuñjīta | jīvati tu tad rakṣed amuktahastatājñāpanārtham | &lt;4.1.10] tathā vyāsavacanam api bhartṛdeyaparyantatājñāpanārtham | yathā,

		dvisahasraḥ paro dāyaḥ striyai deyo dhanasya tu |

		yac ca bhartrā dhanaṃ dattaṃ sā yathākāmam aśnīyāt || [Vy 265]

dvisahasraparyantaḥ striyai deyo nādhikaḥ | kenety ākāṅkṣāyāṃ bharteti śrutam anveti na punar aśrutakalpanā | tathā ca deya iti dadātir mukhyaḥ syāt | mṛtapatidhane tu tāvati patnyā eva svāmitād gauṇaḥ | sa cānyāyyaḥ | [[4.1.11]] yac ca bhartṛdattaṃ dhanaṃ tad yathākāmam aśnīyāt |

	ato 'putrasya mṛtasya patyur dhane dvisahasraparyante patnyā adhikāro na sarvatreti yad uktaṃ tad vidvadbhir anādeyam | [[4.1.12]] etac ca vistareṇa vakṣyate |

[[4.1.13]] āha yājñavalkyaḥ |

		pitṛmātṛpatibhrātṛdattam adhyagnyupāgatam |

		ādhivedanikaṃ caiva strīdhanaṃ parikīrtitam || [Y 2.143]

[[4.1.14]] yac ca dvitīyastrīvivāhārthinā pūrvastriyai pāritoṣikaṃ dhanaṃ dattaṃ tad ādhivedanikam adhikastrīlābhārthatvāt tasya |

[[4.1.15]] tathā devalaḥ |

		vṛttir ābharaṇaṃ śulkaṃ lābaś ca strīdhanaṃ bhavet |

		bhoktrī tat svayam evedaṃ patir nārhaty anāpadi || [D 1604]

[[4.1.16]] tathā vyāsaḥ |

		vivāhakāle yat kiṃcid varāyoddiśya dīyate |

		kanyāyās tad dhanaṃ sarvam avibhājyaṃ ca bandhubhiḥ || [Vy 266]

[[4.1.17]] uddiśyeti kanyāyā idaṃ bhavatv ity uddiśya yad varāya dānaṃ na punar etad abhisaṃdhiṃ vināpīty arthaḥ | ata eva vivāhakāla iti pradarśanārthaṃ na punar etad eva prayojakaṃ dātrabhisaṃdhinimittatvāt svatvasya | tathā prāmāṇikavacanam:

		yad dattaṃ duhituḥ patye striyam eva tad anviyāt |

		mṛte jīvati vā patyau tadapatyam ṛte striyāḥ || [Dhko p. 1463]

vivāhakāla iti na viśinaṣṭi | abhisaṃdhis tu duhitranvayābhidhānād eva labdhatvān noktaḥ |

[[4.1.18]] tad evam avyavasthitasaṃkhyastrīdhanakīrtanān na ṣaṭsaṃkhyā vivakṣitā kiṃ tu strīdhanakīrtanamātraparāṇi munivacanāni | tad eva ca striyā dhanaṃ yatra bhartṛtaḥ svātantryeṇa dānavikrayabhogān kartum adhikaroti |

[[4.1.19]] tad idaṃ saṃkṣipyāha kātyāyanaḥ |

		prāptaṃ śilpais tu yad vittaṃ prītyā caiva yad anyataḥ |

		bhartuḥ svāmyaṃ tadā tatra śeṣaṃ tu strīdhanaṃ smṛtam || [K 904]

&lt;4.1.20] anyata iti pitṛmātṛbhartṛkulavyatiriktād yal labdhaṃ śilpena vā yad arjitaṃ tatra bhartuḥ svāmyaṃ svātantryam | anāpady api bhartā grahītum arhati | tena striyā api dhanaṃ na strīdhanam strīdhanam asvātantryāt |

[[4.1.21]] etaddvayātiriktadhanaṃ tu striyā eva dānavikrayādhikārāt | tad āha kātyāyanaḥ |

		ūḍhayā kanyayā vāpi patyuḥ pitṛgṛhe 'tha vā |

		bhartuḥ sakāśāt pitror vā labdhaṃ saudāyikaṃ smṛtam || [K 901]

		saudāyikaṃ dhanaṃ prāpya strīṇāṃ svātantryam iṣyate |

		yasmāt tadānṛśaṃsyārthaṃ tair dattaṃ tatprajīvanam || [K 905]

		saudāyike sadā strīṇāṃ svātantryaṃ parikīrtitam |

		vikraye caiva dāne ca yatheṣṭaṃ sthāvareṣv api || [K 906]

[[4.1.22]] sudāyasaṃbandhibhyo labdhaṃ saudāyikam |

[[4.1.23]] sthāvare 'pi bhartṛdattamātre striyā na dānādyadhikāraḥ | tad āha nāradaḥ |

		bhratrā prītena yad dattaṃ striyai tasmin mṛte 'pi tat |

		sā yathākāmam aśnīyād dadyād vā sthāvarād ṛte || [N 1.28/24]

bhartṛdattaviśeṣanād bhartṛdattasthāvarād ṛte 'nyat sthāvaraṃ deyam eva bhavati | anyathā yatheṣṭaṃ sthāvareṣv apīti virudhyeta |

[[4.1.24]] bhartā tu yadā durbhikṣādau strīdhanaṃ vinā vartanākṣamas tadāpi grahītum arhati nānyadā | tad āha yājñavalkyaḥ |

		durbhikṣe dharmakārye ca vyādhau saṃpratirodhake |

		gṛhītaṃ strīdhanaṃ bhartā na striyai dātym arhati || [Y 2.147]

anyatra punar anadhikāram āha kātyāyanaḥ |

		na bhartā naiva ca suto na pitā bhrātaro na vā |

		ādāne vā visarge vā strīdhane prabhaviṣṇavaḥ || [K 911]

		yadi hy ekataras tv eṣāṃ strīdhanaṃ bhakṣayed balāt |

		savṛddhiṃ pratidāpyaḥ syād daṇḍaṃ caiva samāpnuyāt || [K 912]

		tad eva yady anujñāpya bhakṣayet prītipūrvakam |

		mūlam eva sa dāpyaḥ syāt yadā sa dhanavān bhavet || [K 913]

		atha cet sa dvibhāryaḥ syān na ca tāṃ bhajate punaḥ |

		prītyā visṛṣṭam api cet pratidāpyaḥ sa tad balāt || [K 908]

		grāsācchādanavāsānām ucchedo yatra yoṣitaḥ |

		tatra svam ādadīta strī vibhāgaṃ rikthinām tathā || [K 909]

[[4.1.25]] striyā dhanaṃ gṛhītvā yady aparabhāryayā saha vasati tāṃ cāvajānīte tadā gṛhītadhanaṃ rājñā balād dāpyaḥ |

	bhaktācchādanādikaṃ yadi bhartā na dadāti tadā tad api striyā ākṛṣya grāhyam |

[[4.1.26]] iti strīdhanalakṣaṇam |

## 02

[[4.2.1]] idānīṃ strīdhanavibhāgo 'bhidhīyate | tatra manuḥ |

		jananyāṃ saṃsthitāyāṃ tu samaṃ sarve sahodarāḥ |

		bhajeran mātṛkaṃ rikthaṃ bhaginyaś ca sanābhayaḥ || [M 9.192]

[[4.2.2]] dvandvāśravaṇe 'pi tattulyārthakacakāreṇa bhrātṛbhaginyor itaretarayuktayor vibhāgapratipādanād bhaginyaḥ sodarāś ca vibhajerann ity ayam evāsya vacanasyārthaḥ |

[[4.2.3]] bṛhaspatir api cakārāt samuccayam āha |

		strīdhanaṃ tadapatyānāṃ duhitā ca tadaṃśinī |

		aprattā cet samūḍhā tu na labhen mātṛkaṃ dhanam || [B 26.31cded]

[[4.2.4]] apatyapadaṃ putraparam | teṣām aprattābhir duhitṛbhiḥ saha mātṛdhanavibhāgaḥ | tathā ca śaṅkhalikhitau |

		samaṃ sarve sodaryā dravyam arhanti kumāryāś ca | [ŚL 297]

[[4.2.5]] sarvatraiva prathamaṃ putropādānāt sarvāvasthasya putrasya mātṛdhane 'dhikāraḥ | cakāraśrutiś ca sarvatrānugatā samuccayavācikā |

[[4.2.6]] etāvatāpy udgrāhamallasya devalavacanaṃ galahastaḥ | yathā,

		sāmānyaṃ putrakanyānāṃ mṛtāyāṃ strīdhanaṃ striyām |

		aprajāyāṃ hared bhartā mātā bhrāta pitāpi vā || [D 1611]

[[4.2.7]] iha putrakanyayoḥ sādhāraṇaṃ mātṛdhanam iti suvyaktam |

	kevalakumāryāḥ sakalamātṛdhanādhikāritve ca yautakadhane viśeṣavacanaṃ manvādīnām anarthakaṃ syāt sarvatrādhikārāviśeṣāt |

[[4.2.8]] yaḥ punar evaṃ samādhānaṃ brūte — bhrātṛbhaginyos tulyavaj jananīdhanādhikāritve samavibhāgitāvidhānaṃ yuktam | kevalabhaginīnāṃ tadabhāve ca kevalabhrārṝṇāṃ dhanasaṃbandhe samaṃ syād aśrutatvād viśeṣasyeti nyāyata eva samatvaprāpter anarthakaṃ samam iti | sa evaṃ vācyaḥ — bhrātṛbhaginyor apy adhikāre samaṃ syād iti nyāyāt samatvaprāpter aviśeṣās ānarthakyasya tadavasthatvāt | kiṃ ca kevalabhrātradhikārapakṣe 'pi pitṛdhana iva mātṛdhane 'pi viṃśoddhārādiprasaktinivartakatayā samapadasya sārthakatvāt katham anarthakatā | ato vacananyāyānabhijñaḥ sarvaiḥ prājñair ajñeya eva kiṃcijjñaḥ |

[[4.2.9]] kiṃtūktād eva hetoḥ putrakumārīduhitros tulyavad adhikāraḥ | etayoś cānyatarābhāve 'nyatarasya tad dhanam | dvayor apy etayor abhāva ūḍhāyā duhituḥ putravatyāḥ saṃbhāvitaputrāyāś ca tulyo 'dhikāraḥ svaputradvāreṇa pārvaṇapiṇḍadānasaṃbhavāt | [[4.2.10]] ata eva pūrvoktaduhitrabhāve dauhitrasyaiva dhanādhikāraḥ —

		dauhitre 'pi hy amutrainaṃ saṃtārayati pautravat || [M 9.139cd]

iti manuvacanāt | na tu vandhyāvidhavāduhitroḥ svasattayā svajanyasattayā ca pārvaṇapiṇḍadānābhāvāt | ata eva nāradaḥ |

		putrābhāve tu duhitā tulyasaṃtānadarśanāt | [N 13.50/47ab]

[[4.2.11]] pautrdauhitrayos tu sadbhāve pautrasyādhikāraḥ putreṇa pariṇītaduhitur bādhād bādhakaputreṇa bādhyaduhitṛputrabādhasya nyāyyatvāt |

**	**[[4.2.12]] uktānāṃ tu sarveṣāṃ dauhitraparyantānām abhāve vandhyāvidhavayor api mātṛdhanādhikāritā tayor api tatprajātvāt prajābhāve cānyeṣām adhikārāt |

[[4.2.13]] yat tu duhitṛmātrādhikārārthaṃ gautamavacanam: "strīdhanaṃ duhitṝṇāṃ aprattānām apratiṣṭhitānāṃ ca" [G 28.24] | yac ca nāradasya,

		mātur duhitaro 'bhāve duhitṝṇāṃ tadanvayaḥ | [N 13.2cd]

yac ca kātyāyanasyāpi,

		duhitṝṇām abhāve tu rikthaṃ putreṣu tad bhavet | [K 918ab]

tathā yājñavalkyasya,

		mātur duhitaraḥ śeṣam ṛṇāt tābhya ṛte 'nvayaḥ | [Y 2.117cd]

tāni pūrvoktadevalavacanavirodhena yautakadravyaviṣayāṇi | ata eva manuḥ —

		mātus tu yautakaṃ yat syāt kumārībhāga eva saḥ | [M 9.131ab]

[[4.2.14]] yautakaṃ pariṇayanalabdham | yu miśraṇa iti dhātor yutapadaṃ miśratāvacanam | miśratā ca strīpuruṣayor ekaśarīratā vivāhāc ca tad bhavati, "asthibhir asthīni māṃsair māṃsāni tvacā tvacam" [Pāraskara GṛS 1.11.5] iti śruteḥ | ato vivāhakāle labdhaṃ yautakam |

[[4.2.15]] ata eva vasiṣṭhaḥ: "mātuḥ pāriṇāyyaṃ striyo vibhajeran" [Va 17.46] | pāriṇāyyaṃ pariṇayanalabdhaṃ dhanam |

[[4.2.16]] yat tu manuvacanam,

		striyās tu yad bhavad vittaṃ pitrā dattaṃ kathaṃcana |

		brāhmaṇī tad dharet kanyā tadapatyasya vā bhavet || [M 9.198]

atra pitrā dattam iti viśeṣaṇād vivāhasamayād anyatrāpi yat pitṛdattaṃ tat kanyāyā evety etadartham | brāhmaṇīpadaṃ cānuvādaḥ | yad vā brāhmaṇīpadasyānarthakyabhayāt kṣatriyādistrīṇām anapatyānāṃ pitṛdattaṃ dhanaṃ sapatnīduhitā brāhmaṇī kanyā haret | na punar aprajāstrīdhanaṃ bhartur iti vacanāvakāśa iti vacanārthaḥ | anyathā sakalavacanānām asāmañjasyaṃ syāt |

[[4.2.17]] na ca vācyam — nāradādibhir duhitur abhāve duhituḥ putrāṇām eva dhanādhikāro darśitaḥ pratyasannaduhitṛpadenaivānvayapadasyānvayād iti | 

yato duhitṛpadasyānvayapadasya ca janyaviśeṣaparatvena janakākāṅkṣitatvān na janyāntareṇānvayapadopāttena putreṇānvayaḥ saṃbhavati samatvāt | [[4.2.18]] na cādhiṣṭhānalakṣaṇayānvayo vācyaḥ mātranvayenaiva sarveṣāṃ mukhyatvasaṃbhavāt | mātṛpadānvaye ca duhitṛpadasya mukhyatvasvīkārāt | [[4.2.19]] na ca tadanvaya iti tacchabdopāttāyā duhitur anvayayogyatā vācyā tacchabdasyāpi prakṛtavācitayā duhitṛtvarūpeṇaivopapādakatvāt |

[[4.2.20]] kiṃ ca yājñavalkyavacane duhitara iti padaṃ prathamāntaṃ tābhya iti padaṃ pañcamyantam anvayapadena ṣaṣṭyantānvayayogyena nānvīyate | kiṃ tu vyavahitam api mātur ity eva padam anvayi | tad atra mātur anvaye niścite nārada-kātyāyanavākye 'pi mātur evānvayo nyāyyaḥ avirodhāt |

[[4.2.21]] kiṃ ca, "satsv aṅgajeṣu tadgāmī hy artho bhavati" [Bau 1.5.11.11] iti baudhāyanavacanusāreṇānantaryāc cāṅgajaputrasyādhikāro nyāyyo nānaṅgajavyavahitadauhitrādhikāraḥ | 

[[4.2.22]] tataś ca pariṇayanalabdhaṃ strīdhanaṃ duhitur eva na purāṇām | tatraiva ca kramārthaṃ gotamavacanam: "strīdhanaṃ duhitṛṇām aprattānām apratiṣṭhitānāṃ ca" [G 28.24] |

[[4.2.23]] prathamam aprattānāṃ tadabhāve prattānāṃ ca samūḍhānām | strīdhanaṃ duhitṝṇām iti sāmānyataḥ prāptatvād aprattānām ityādes tu kramārthatvenopasaṃhārārthatvāt |

[[4.2.24]] tathā yājñavalkyaḥ |

		aprajāstrīdhanaṃ bhartur brāhmādiṣu caturṣv api |

		duhitṝṇāṃ prasūtā cec cheṣeṣu pitṛgāmi tat || [Y 2.145]

[[4.2.25]]brāhmādivivāheṣu yal labdham adhyagnidhanaṃ striyā tat tasyāṃ mṛtāyāṃ prathamaṃ duhitṝṇām eva | tatrāpi prathamaṃ kanyāyās tadabhāve prattāyās tadabhāve pariṇītāyāḥ | sarvaduhitrabhāve putrasyādhikāraḥ aprajāstrīdhane bhartur adhikārāt | 

[[4.2.26]] bṛhaspatina tv aprattāpadenāprattādyabhāve samūḍhāyā apy adhikāraḥ sūcitaḥ |

[[4.2.27]] na ca yautakamātradhanābhiprāyeṇa nedaṃ vacanam | kiṃ tu brāhmādivivāhena vivāhitāyā yad yāvad dhanaṃ yautakam ayautakaṃ vā tadabhiprāyeṇeti vācyam, bandhudattam iti vacanasya nirviṣayatvāpatteḥ, manuvirodhāc ca | yad āha |

		brāhmadaivārṣagāndharvaprājāpatyeṣu yad dhanam |

		atītāyām aprajāyāṃ bhartur eva tad iṣyate || [M 9.196]

		yat tv asyāḥ syād dhanaṃ dattaṃ vivāheṣv āsurādiṣu |

		atītāyām aprajāyāṃ mātāpitros tad iṣyate || [M 9.197]

asyāḥ syād dattam iti prācīnaṃ pūrvatrānuṣajyate | tena vivāheṣu yad dhanaṃ dattam iti saṃbandhād vaivāhikadhanamātrapratīter na yāvaddhanaviṣayatvam  |

[[4.2.28]] tathā yamaḥ |

		āsurādiṣu yad dravyaṃ vivāheṣu pradīyate | [Dhko p. 1462]

vivāhakriyāyāṃ pūrvāparībhūtāyāṃ yad dravyaṃ pradīyata it yautakadhanamātragocaratvam eva pratīyate |

[[4.2.29]] na ca vivāhāt pūrvaṃ parato vā striyā labdhasyāprajāstrīdhanasya gater aśrūyamāṇatvād brāhmādipadaṃ strīparam iti vācyam | pūrvāparalabdhasya vistareṇa gater vakṣyamāṇatvāt |

## 03

[[4.3.1]] saṃpraty aprajāstrīdhane 'dhikāriṇaḥ kathyante |

[[4.3.2]] tatra yājñavalkyaḥ |
