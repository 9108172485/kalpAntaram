## 01
[[6.1.1]] saṃprati vibhājyam avibhājyaṃ cocyate | tatra kātyāyanaḥ |

		paitāmahaṃ ca pitryaṃ ca yac cānyat svayam arjitam |

		dāyādānāṃ vibhāge tu sarvam etad vibhajyate || [K 840]

[[6.1.2]] yac cānyad iti cakāraḥ svayam ity anena saṃbadhyate svayaṃ cārjitam iti | cakārād anyasyāpi tadarjanaṃ sādhāraṇadhanadvāreṇety arthaḥ ||

[[6.1.3]] anupaghātopāttam avibhājyam āhatur manu-viṣṇū |

		anupaghnan pitṛdravyaṃ śrameṇa yad upārjayet |

		svayam īhitalabdhaṃ tan nākāmo dātum arhati || [M9.208 = Vi 18.42]

[[6.1.4]] pitṛdravyopaghātābhāvena dravyadvāreṇa netareṣāṃ vyāpāraḥ svaceṣṭālabdhatvena śārīro 'pi vyāpāro netareṣām ity arjakasyaiva tad asādhāraṇaṃ svayam īhitalabdhaṃ tad iti hetutvenopanyāsāt | [[6.1.5]] tathā vyāsaḥ |

		anāśritya pitṛdravyaṃ svaśaktyāpnoti yad dhanam |

		dāyādebhyo na tad dadyād vidyālabdhaṃ tu tad bhavet || [Vy 256]

[[6.1.6]] svaśaktimātreṇa yat prāptam iti sāmānyenābhidhānāt sarvam evaṃvidham asādhāraṇaṃ dravyam | svaśaktiprāptasyāpi vidyādhanasya samānādhikavidyaiḥ sādhāraṇatvān nyūnavidyāvidyanirākaraṇārthaṃ vidyālabdhapadam | [[6.1.7]] tathā yājñavalkyaḥ |

		pitṛdravyāvirodhena yad anyat svayam arjitam |

		maitram audvāhikaṃ caiva dāyādānāṃ na tad bhavet || [Y 2.118]

[[6.1.9]] tathā manuḥ |

		vidyādhanaṃ tu yad yadya tat tasyaiva dhanaṃ bhavet |

		maitram audvāhikaṃ caiva mādhupārkikam eva ca || [M 9.206]

[[6.1.10]] tathā ca vyāsaḥ |

		vidyāprāptaṃ śauryadhanaṃ yac ca saudāyikaṃ bhavet |

		vibhāgakāle tat tasya nanveṣṭavyaṃ svarikthibhiḥ || [Vy 257]

[[6.1.11]] pitṛpitṛvyādibhyaḥ sudāyasaṃndhibhyaḥ prasādādinā labdhaṃ saudāyikam |

[[6.1.12]] tathā nāradaḥ |

		śauryabhāryādhane hitvā yac ca vidyādhanaṃ bhavet |

		trīṇy etāny avibhājyāni prasādo yaś ca paitṛkaḥ || [N 13.6]

[[6.1.13]] bhāryāprāptikāle labdhaṃ bhāryādhanam audvāhikam ity arthaḥ | etāni varjayitvā anyat | vibhajed ity anuvartate vākyāntarīyam |

[[6.1.14]] tad evamādibhiḥ śauryādidhanatvam avibhājyatve kāraṇaṃ nocyate | śauryādyarjitasyāpi vibhāgaśruteḥ | tad āha vyāsaḥ |

		sādhāraṇaṃ samāśritya yat kiṃcid vāhanāyudham |

		śauryādināpnoti dhanaṃ bhrātaras tatra bhāginaḥ || [Vy 259]

		tasya bhāgadvayaṃ deyaṃ śeṣās tu samabhāginaḥ || [Vy 260]

sādhāraṇadravyeṇārjitasya dhanasya vibhāgaṃ vadati | tathā nāradaḥ |

		kuṭumbaṃ bibhṛyād bhratur yo vidyām adhigacchataḥ |

		bhagaṃ vidyādhanāt tasmāt sa labhetāśruto 'pi san || [N 13.10]

[[6.1.15]] bibhṛyād ity ekavacananirdeśād yadi vidyām abhyasyato bhrātuḥ kuṭumbam aparo bhrātā svadhanavyayaśarīrāyāmābhyāṃ saṃvardhayati tadā tadvidyopārjitadhane tasyāpy adhikāraḥ | [[6.1.16]] tathā |

		vaidyo 'vidyāyā nākāmo dadyād aṃśaṃ svato dhanāt |

		pitryaṃ dravyaṃ samāśritya na cet tena tad arjitam || [N 13.11]

[[6.1.17]] pitryapadaṃ sādhāraṇaparam | tadanāśrityārjitaṃ vaidyo 'vidyāyānicchan na dadyāt | vaidyāya viduṣe punaḥ sādhāraṇadhanam antareṇāpy arjitaṃ dadyād eva | tathā gautamaḥ: "svayam arjitam avaidyebhyo vaidyo 'kāmaṃ na dadyāt" [G 28.30] | [[6.1.18]] asādhāraṇadhanaśarīravyāpārārjitaṃ svayam arjitam avidvadbhyo dātum anicchan na dadyād vidvadbhyaḥ punar dadyād eva |

[[6.1.19]] etac ca vidyādhanamātraviṣayam | tad āha kātyāyanaḥ |

		nāvidyānāṃ tu vaidyena deyaṃ vidyādhanāt kvacit |

		samavidhādhikānāṃ tu deyaṃ vaidyena tad dhanam || [K 875]

[[6.1.20]] tantroccaritavidyāpadam ubhābhyāṃ samādhikapadābhyāṃ saṃbadhyate | tena samavidyādhikavidyānāṃ dātavyam | nyūnavidyāvidyayoḥ punar anadhikāraḥ |

[[6.1.21]] tad evamādivacanair vidyāśauryādidhaneṣv api sādhāraṇadhanopaghātānupaghātābhyāṃ vibhāgāvibhāgayor avagamāt tasyaiva prayojakatvāt tatpadavaty eva śrutiḥ kalpanīyā upaghātārjitaṃ vibhajed iti | na punaḥ śauryādipadavaty api avaśyakalpanīyasāmānyaśrutikalpanayaivopapatteḥ | [[6.1.22]] holākādhikaraṇsyāyam eva viṣayaḥ |

[[6.1.23]] yad vā nyāyaprāta evāyam arthaḥ — yad yenārjitaṃ tat tasmin jīvati tasyaivāsati viśeṣavacane | yatra punaḥ sādhāraṇadhanamātreṇaikasya vyāpādo 'parasya dhanaśarīrābhyāṃ tatraikasyaiko bhāgo 'parasya bhāgadvayaṃ nyāyāvagatam eva nibaddham | etena caitad api sidhyati yat sādhāraṇadhanopaghāte yasya yāvato 'ṃśasyālpasya mahato vopaghātas tasya tadanusāreṇa bhāgakalpanā kāryā |

[[6.1.24]] kiṃ ca kātyāyanavacanam |

		vibhaktāḥ pitṛvittāc ced ekatra prativāsinaḥ |

		vibhajeyuḥ punar dvyaṃśaṃ sa labhetodayo yataḥ || [K 892]

[[6.1.25]] idaṃ saṃsṛṣṭasya sādhāraṇadhanopaghātenārjakasya bhāgadvayam itareṣām ekaiko bhāga iti śrīkareṇa vyākhyātam | [[6.1.26]] tenānupaghātārjitam arjakasyaiva | saṃsṛṣṭatve 'pi na punar tad dhanaṃ sādhāraṇam ity abhiprāyo muner vyākhyātuś ca lakṣyate anupaghātārjite bhāvaviśeṣānabhidhānāt |

[[6.1.27]] evaṃ cet saṃsṛṣṭavad avibhaktasyāpi tathātvam eva yuktam | vibhāgaprāgabhāve tatpradhvaṃse 'py ekatra prativāsasya hetor aviśeṣāt | sādhāraṇadhanopaghātārjite 'rjakasya bhāgadvayam iti jñāpanārthatvena vacanasyāpy upapatteḥ | na kevalaṃ saṃsṛṣṭaviṣayatvaṃ yuktaṃ holākādhikaraṇasyātraiva jāgarūkatvāt | 

[[6.1.28]] kiṃ copaghātārjite tāvad arjakasya bhāgadvayam iti nirvivādam,

		sādhāraṇaṃ samāśritya yat kiṃcid vāhanāyudham |

		śauryādināprāpnoti dhanaṃ bhrātaras tatra bhāginaḥ || [Vy 259]

		tasya bhāgadvayaṃ deyaṃ śeṣāś tu samabhāginaḥ | [Vy 260]

ity anenopaghāta eva bhāgadvayavidhānāt | asādhāraṇadhanaśarīravyāpāramātrārjite tu na bhāgadvayaṃ nyāyyam | kiṃ tv adhikaṃ sarvam eva vā kiṃcidūnaṃ vā | tatra kiṃcidūnasya munibhir nibandhṛbhiś cānuktatvāt sādhāraṇadhanavyāpāraṇe bhrātrantarabhāganirgamāt tadabhāve bhāgābhāva eva yuktaḥ |

[[6.1.29]] dvir arjayitur ity etasya ca nyāyamūlatvam eva yuktam | anyathā śrutikalpane 'rjakatvānupraveśo vā pṛthag vādhikārī kalpanīyaḥ syāt |

[[6.1.30]] tasmād anupaghārjitam arjakasyaiva netareṣām iti siddham |

[[6.1.31]] kiṃ cāvibhaktārjitaṃ sarve vibhajeyur iti na tāvat sāmānyena vacanaṃ kalpanīyaṃ śauryādidhanaparyudāsadarśanāt | tathā manuḥ |

		vidyādhanaṃ tu yad yasya tat tasyaiva dhanaṃ bhavet |

		maitram audvāhikaṃ caiva mādhuparkikam eva ca || [M 9.206]

tathā manu-viṣṇū |

		anupaghnan pitṛdravyaṃ śrameṇa yad upārjayet |

		svayam īhitalabdhaṃ tan nākāmo dātum arhati || [M 9.208 = Vi 18.42]

[[6.1.32]] anupagnann iti vidhādhane 'pi saṃbadhyate saty upaghāte vibhāgavacanadarśanāt |

[[6.1.33]] tathā yājñavalkyaḥ |

		pitṛdravyāvināśena yad anyat svayam arjitam |

		maitram audvāhikaṃ caiva dāyādānāṃ na tad bhavet || [Y 2.118]

		kramād abhyāgataṃ dravyaṃ hṛtam abhyuddharet tu yaḥ |

		dāyādebhyo na tad dadyād vidyayā labdham eva ca || [Y 2.119]

tathā nāradaḥ |

		śauryabhāryādhane hitvā yac ca vidhyādhanaṃ bhavet |

		trīṇy etāny avibhājyāni prasādo yaś ca paitṛkaḥ || [N 13.6]

tathā vyāsaḥ |

		vidyāprāptaṃ śauryadhanaṃ yac ca saudāyikaṃ bhavet |

		vibhāgakāle tat tasya nānveṣṭavyaṃ svarikthibhiḥ || [Vy 257]

[[6.1.34]] saudāyikaṃ sudāyasaṃbandhibhyo yal labdham |

[[6.1.35]]	pitāmahena yad dattaṃ pitrā vā prītipūrvakam |

		tasya tan nāpahartavyaṃ mātrā dattaṃ ca yad bhavet || [Vy 261 ]

		anāśritya pitṛdravyaṃ svaśaktyāpnoti yad dhanam |

		dāyādebhyo na tad dadyād vidyālabdhaṃ ca yad bhavet || [Vy 256]

[[6.1.36]] tad evamādivacanair yāvad varṇavarṇāntarālānāṃ saṃkīrṇajātānāṃ sakalavidyānimittasya saudāyikasya ca svajanadattasya tathā maitravivāhamadhuparkaprāptasya śauryeṇa ca yuddhādinā prāptasya kṛṣisevāvāṇijyādinā ca śrameṇārjitasyānupaghātena ca svaśaktimātrārjitasya paryudāsāt sarvam eva paryudastam iti tad itarābhāvān nirviṣayo vidhiḥ |

[[6.1.37]] atha yathākathaṃcid eko dviko vā viṣayo labhyate tadā tad eva svapadena nirdeṣṭum ucitaṃ munīnām | avibhaktārjitam amukadhanaṃ vibhajed iti lāghavāt | svapadāc ca śīghrapratīteḥ | na tu sauryādidhanetaratayā bahutarapadaprayogāpattyā gauravāt |

	paryudāsatve ca sarvamunibhir eva sakalaparyudāsanīyapadānukīrtanaṃ kartavyaṃ tadvinā taditarajñānānupapatteḥ | munīnāṃ paryudāsavacanaṃ bālapralapitam iva syāt | pradarśanārthatve tv anāsthayā kenacit kiṃcit kīrtanaṃ kenacic ca kiṃcid iti yuktaṃ sarvasyākīrtanam |

[[6.1.38]] tasmāt sādhāraṇadhanopaghātārjitaṃ dhanaṃ vibhajed iti vidhiḥ | śauryādipadaṃ ca vākyeṣu pradarśanārtham | [[6.1.39]] ato 'vibhktārjitatvamātreṇa dhanasya sādharaṇatvābhidhānam aprāmāṇikam |

[[6.1.40]] kiṃ ca |

		kramād abhyāgataṃ dravyaṃ hṛtam abhyuddharet tu yaḥ |

		dāyādebhyo na tad dadyād vidyayā labdham eva ca || [Y 2.119]

atra yājñavalkyavacane pitṛpitāmahādidhanam api kenacid apahṛtaṃ yo 'bhyuddharet tasyaiva tan nānyeṣām iti bhavato 'pi saṃmatam | tena pūrvasaṃbandhaleśe saty apy vibhaktānām apy abhyuddhārakatvena tatra saṃbandhaṃ nirākurvan na pūrvatvena svārjite sudūram evānyeṣāṃ saṃbandhaṃ nirasyati |

[[6.1.41]] yac coktaṃ śrīkareṇa — "yadi pitṛdravyānupaghātārjitam arjakasyaiva tadā pratigrahopāttaṃ dhanaṃ na kadācid bhrātrantarasya bhavet | na hi pratigrahaḥ pitṛdravyavināśena saṃbhavati | dravyaṃ hi dātur ānatidvārā pratigraha upayujyate | ekahāyanyādikam iva somakraye kartṛśarīradhāraṇena vā payovratādikam iva jyotiṣṭome | tatra tāvad adṛṣṭārthadāne dravyānatragrahaṇena na dātur ānatir apekṣiteti na dātrānatyā dravyam upayujyate | pratigrahasya cālpakālīnatvān na tatkartur bhōjanam apekṣitam | dīrghakālīnajyotiṣṭomeneva svargakatuḥ" iti | 

[[6.1.42]] tan mandam | dāpakān atyartham upahārapradānādinā dhanopaghātasya loke bahulam upalambhāt | kalau ca pratigrahadhanasya sevādhanasamānatvāt | ata eva "kalau tv anugamānvitaḥ" iti smaranti |

[[6.1.43]] yac ca — cirāvasthiter vyabhicārān na pratigrahakāraṇatvam ānater ata ānatidvārā na pratigrahārthatvaṃ dravyasyety uktam |

tan mandataram | ānatidvāreṇa cirāśrayaṇādīnāṃ pratigrahakāraṇatvāt puruṣāśayavaicitryeṇa kasyacid dhanadānena kasyacic cirāśrayaṇādinā kasyacit tattadguṇānusaṃdhānamātreṇa darśanāt | sahakāryabhāvena kāryānutpatter nākāraṇatā | ata evoktam, ānater aniyatopāyapariṇāmatvāt | 

[[6.1.44]] tad apy ayuktam — atha tatsaṃnidhim antareṇa pratigrahasyāsaṃbhavād bhojanam antareṇa ca tadayogāt tasyāṃ sthitau vyāpriyamāṇaṃ dhanaṃ praṇālyā pratigrahaṃ niṣpādayatīti | tadā jyotiṣṭomādikarmaṇaḥ prācīnam api bhojanaṃ śarīrasthitau vyāpriyamāṇaṃ prācīnaśarīrasthitim antareṇa jyotiṣṭomāniṣpatteḥ praṇālyā jyotiṣṭomārtham iti sarvam eva bhojanaṃ kratvarthaṃ syān na puruṣārtham | tathā ca tatsādhanam api yad dravyaṃ tat kratvarthaṃ syāt | yadarjanopāyo 'pi kratvarthaḥ syād iti puruṣārthatvaṃ dravyārjanasya dravyasya bhojanasya ca hīyateti |

[[6.1.45]] tan mandatamam | praṇālyā jyotiṣṭomopakārakatve 'pi bhojanasya sākṣāt tṛptyarthatvāt | puruṣārthasyaiva sataḥ kratūpakārakatvāt tatraidamarthe pramāṇābhāvāt | upakārakatvasya tādarthyavyabhicārāt | ataḥ kathaṃ dravyārjanasya dravyasya bhojanasya ca kratvarthatvam āpadyeta |

[[6.1.46]] ata evāsyāpi paryanuyogasyānavakāśaḥ | yadi dravyasya pratigrahe prācīnabhojanadvārā pratigrahopakārakatvam iṣyate tadā janmata ārabhya bhojanaṃ vinā śarīrāvasthiter abhāvān nārjanaṃ saṃbhavatīti sarva eva dhanopayaḥ pitṛdravyavināśena syāt | ato "anupaghnan pitṛdravyam" iti viśeṣaṇaṃ na syād iti | yato viśeṣanānarthakyād eva bhakṣaṇādyupabhogopayuktadhanopaghātād anyasyaivopaghātādirūpasya vacanārthatvāt |

[[6.1.47]] kiṃ ca bhakṣaṇādyupabhogārthaṃ dhanopaghātasya gṛhagatenāpy avaśyakartavyatvān na dhanārjanārthatvam upaghātasya | tādarthyam eva ca prayojakam iti nātiprasaktiḥ |

[[6.1.53]] yaś cānupaghātapratigrahārjitadhanasya vibhāgaḥ śiṣṭānāṃ dṛśyate sa bhrātṛsnehena vā pauruṣabuddhyā vā nānupapannaḥ |

## 02
[[6.2.1]] tatra vidyādhanaṃ tāvad abhidhīyate | tatra kātyāyanaḥ |

		upanyaste tu yal labdhaṃ vidyayā paṇapūrvakam |

		vidyādhanaṃ tu tad vidyād vibhāge na niyojayet || [K 868]

		śiṣyād ārtvijyataḥ praśnāt saṃdigdhapraśnanirṇayāt |

		svajñānaśaṃsanād vādāl labdhaṃ prādhyayanāc ca yat || [K 869abcd]

		vidhyādhanaṃ tu tat prāhur vibhāge na vibhajyate || [K 869ef]

		śilpeṣv api hi dharmo 'yaṃ mūlyād yac cādhikaṃ bhavet || K 870]

		paraṃ narasya yal labdhaṃ vidyayā dyūtapūrvakam |
